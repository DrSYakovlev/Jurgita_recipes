Recipe 1 - Schi (4 portions)

Ingredients:

  • 1 large potatoe: peeled and cubed
  • 2-3 carrots: peeled and grated (coarse)
  • 500 g of sauerkraut
  • Knorr pot: vegetable and chicken (one each)
  • 1.5 L boiling water
  • Salt,pepper (pepper corns), 1-2 bay leaves
  • Finely chopped garlic to serve

Actions to get there:

  1. Boil the water, add Knorr pots, bay leaves and pepper. Make sure the pots dissolve completely.
  2. Add potatoes and carrots, bring to the boil, cook 7-10 min.
  3. Add souerkraut with the broth, mix well, bring to the boil. Reduce the heat, cover half way pan. Simmer for 30 min
  4. Top up with boiling water if needed.
